# Lessons we should apply (and what to avoid)

Reviewed: 2026-03-13 (Africa/Cairo)

## What consistently works on top-performing agency pages

1) **One clear promise + one clear CTA**
- Hero = outcome (not services) + 1–2 proof points + a single primary CTA (Book a call / WhatsApp).

2) **Proof early, not late**
- “Badges / ratings / logos” appear above or immediately after the hero (Disruptive, SmartSites, Taktical, Voy).

3) **Process beats paragraphs**
- Stepper (“Kickoff → Audience → Creative → Tracking → Testing → Retargeting → Optimization”) builds trust fast (Disruptive/Taktical/Sociallyin).

4) **Productized offers qualify leads**
- Packages (Starter / Growth / Scale) with inclusions reduce back-and-forth (LYFE is very explicit about packages/pricing).

5) **Case studies with context**
- Best case studies include: objective, constraint, what changed, and a KPI + timeframe (Taktical, Sociallyin).

6) **Tracking + transparency**
- Multiple sites explicitly sell “technical audit / pixel / tracking / reporting” as a deliverable (Taktical, SmartSites, WebFX, Thrive).

## Common mistakes to avoid (conversion + trust)

- **Too many CTAs** (every section has a different ask) → decision fatigue.
- **Generic claims** (“best agency”, “guaranteed ROAS”) without proof → skepticism + compliance risk.
- **Long walls of SEO text** without scannable blocks (some pages drift into “guide” format).
- **Hidden ownership** (client should own ad accounts/pages; clarity prevents trust issues).

## Recommended section map for Marketera (landing page)

1. Hero + WhatsApp + Book call
2. Proof bar (rating, achievements, logos)
3. Services (what we do) + “what you get” deliverables
4. Packages (“products”)
5. Achievements (real numbers only)
6. Case studies
7. Reviews
8. Our process (5–7 steps)
9. FAQ (pricing, contract, account ownership, reporting cadence)
10. Contact (phone/WhatsApp/email/form)

## How to adapt these lessons to a Human Therapy website (important differences)

Therapy sites convert on **safety + clarity**, not “aggressive performance”.

Borrow from agency pages:
- Clear hero promise + single calming CTA (Book session / Call / WhatsApp).
- Proof early, but use **clinical/social proof**: credentials, licensing, association memberships, clinic policies, reviews (where allowed).
- Simple stepper (How it works) reduces anxiety: “Choose therapist → Book → First session → Follow-up plan”.
- FAQ is critical: confidentiality, pricing, insurance, online vs in-person, crisis resources.

Avoid importing from agency pages:
- Hype language (“guaranteed results”, “#1”) and loud colors.
- Over-animation; keep page calm, accessible, fast.
- Before/after style claims unless ethically and legally appropriate.
