# Review: Voy Media — “#1 Facebook Ads Agency” landing/service page

URL: https://voymedia.com/
Reviewed: 2026-03-13 (Africa/Cairo)

## Positioning

- Direct response + customer acquisition focus with broad creative-studio offering.

## Layout / sections observed

- Hero (“#1 Facebook Ads Agency”) + CTA
- “Who we’ve worked with” logo strip
- Overview blocks (Advertising / Creative Studio / Growth Marketing / Ventures)
- “Why Voy Media?” narrative + service bullets (full-service FB/IG, retargeting, creative studio, analytics, etc.)
- Company stats block (revenue generated, etc.)
- Testimonials section (multiple quotes)
- Content/brand extensions (podcast, courses, book)

## UI / UX notes

- Feels like a blended landing page + content hub (podcast/book/courses).
- Strong breadth story, but less “tight” as a single conversion-focused landing page.

## What to copy (pattern)

- Simple service bullet list for clarity.
- Strong “what makes us different” headline block.

## What to avoid

- “#1” claim is high-risk without clear proof; we should avoid similar superlatives.

## Transferable lesson for a Human Therapy website

- Avoid “ranked #1” language; but you *can* build trust with credentials, approach, and testimonials (where appropriate).
