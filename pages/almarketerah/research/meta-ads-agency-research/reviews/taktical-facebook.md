# Review: Taktical Digital — Facebook Ads Agency

URL: https://taktical.co/facebook-advertising-agency/
Reviewed: 2026-03-13 (Africa/Cairo)

## Positioning

- “High-performance Meta growth” with heavy use of stats + a very explicit process.

## Layout / sections observed

- Hero + CTA (“Grow with Facebook Ads”)
- Logo wall (“Meet a few of our friends”)
- “Why do you need a Facebook Ads agency?” with platform stats
- “Taktical approach” stepper:
  1. Kickoff & discovery
  2. Audience creation
  3. Creative (monthly new assets)
  4. Technical audit (pixel/tracking)
  5. Testing
  6. Retargeting
  7. Optimization
  8. CRO
- Embedded case study with structure (who they are, goals, process, challenges)
- “Comprehensive marketing services” cross-sell grid
- Awards wall + CTA

## UI / UX notes

- One of the strongest “process” layouts: clear, scannable, and confidence-building.
- Case study storytelling is well-structured and skimmable.

## What to copy (pattern)

- 6–8 step “how we work” section.
- Case study template (Who / Goal / Process / Challenge / Result).

## What to avoid

- Overusing platform market stats can become noise; keep only the most relevant 2–3.

## Transferable lesson for a Human Therapy website

- A step-by-step “what happens next” section is exactly what anxious users need before booking.
