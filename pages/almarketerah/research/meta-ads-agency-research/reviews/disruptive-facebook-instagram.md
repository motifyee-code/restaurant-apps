# Review: Disruptive Advertising — Facebook & Instagram Ads Agency

URL: https://disruptiveadvertising.com/social-media-advertising/facebook-instagram-advertising-agency/
Reviewed: 2026-03-13 (Africa/Cairo)

## Positioning

- “Free audit” as the main lead magnet + strong trust story (ratings, longevity, managed spend).

## Layout / sections observed

- Hero promise + “Get started”
- Pain/diagnosis hook (“lost conversions due to poor strategy”) + “Get your free audit”
- Rotating testimonial snippets near the top
- Trust metrics grid (rating, clients retained, employees, managed spend, etc.)
- “Next steps” stepper (Request audit → Meet team → Start)
- “Meta Business Partner” trust block + honesty statement (“we’ll tell you if we can’t win”)
- Creative emphasis section (prospecting vs remarketing creative)
- “Seen on” media logos
- Industry/client scenario mini-cases
- Methodology (Test / Analyze / Optimize)
- Reviews hub links
- Footer contact info

## UI / UX notes

- High trust density without requiring deep scrolling (good).
- Stepper is a clean way to set expectations and reduce friction.
- Strong “adult in the room” voice: transparency + auditing.

## What to copy (pattern)

- “3-step next steps” section (very effective for lead gen).
- Clear partner/badge block (only if you can legitimately claim it).
- Explicit creative guidance (prospecting vs remarketing) to show expertise.

## What to avoid

- Large numeric claims without context can invite skepticism—add timeframe and methodology if we do similar.

## Transferable lesson for a Human Therapy website

- A simple stepper (“Book → First session → Plan”) reduces fear and increases conversion.
- “If we’re not a fit, we’ll tell you” honesty statement builds trust in healthcare contexts.
