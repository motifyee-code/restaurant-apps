# Review: SmartSites — Facebook Ads Management

URL: https://www.smartsites.com/ppc-marketing/facebook-ads-management/
Reviewed: 2026-03-13 (Africa/Cairo)

## Positioning

- Partner/badge-heavy credibility + immediate lead-capture form.

## Layout / sections observed

- Hero with phone number visible + promise + badges (Meta Business Partner + other partners)
- Multi-step “Grow Your Brand” form above the fold
- “Free PPC analysis” offer (audit lead magnet)
- “Benefits of Facebook Ads Management” section (granularity, analytics, speed…)
- “What we handle” deliverables list (audiences, pixel, reporting, messaging…)
- Large testimonial carousel section with named reviewers

## UI / UX notes

- Extremely lead-gen focused: form-first approach.
- Heavy trust density through badges and review counts.
- Feels “high-intent” rather than inspirational.

## What to copy (pattern)

- Above-the-fold form (if we can keep it short).
- Badges + review count near hero (only if true).
- Clear deliverables list (“we handle X”).

## What to avoid

- Overloading badges can look spammy; pick only the top 3 trust signals.

## Transferable lesson for a Human Therapy website

- Short intake form above the fold can work, but therapy needs stronger privacy reassurance and minimal fields.
