# Review: Ignite Visibility — Facebook Marketing Services

URL: https://ignitevisibility.com/services/facebook-marketing/
Reviewed: 2026-03-13 (Africa/Cairo)

## Positioning

- “Highly awarded” framing + quick promise: custom Facebook strategy.

## Layout / sections observed

- Hero (service name + CTA: request proposal + turnaround expectation)
- “Highly awarded” badge strip
- “Facebook Marketing Services Include” cards (content creation, influencer management, community management, posting schedule, targeted ads, account management, …)

## UI / UX notes

- Clean service-card approach: easy scanning, good for broad audience.
- Strong “proposal” CTA early.

## What to copy (pattern)

- Service list as icon cards with short descriptions.
- Fast “request proposal” module above the fold.

## What to avoid

- “Award wall” is useful, but only if the awards are credible and verifiable.

## Transferable lesson for a Human Therapy website

- “What we do” cards map well to therapy offerings (individual therapy, couples, CBT, anxiety, etc.) if kept non-clinical and clear.
