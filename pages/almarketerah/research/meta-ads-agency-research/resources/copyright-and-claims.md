# Copyright + claims checklist (for Marketera + any client site)

Reviewed: 2026-03-13 (Africa/Cairo)

## Copyright basics (practical)

- Don’t copy competitor copywriting, icons, or unique layout compositions.
- Use licensed images/videos (own content, paid stock, or properly licensed free stock).
- If using Meta/Facebook names/logos, follow brand guidelines and avoid implying endorsement.

## Claims & proof

- Avoid “guaranteed ROAS/CPA” or “#1” unless you can prove it and keep proof available.
- If you show results, add context (industry, timeframe, spend range) and clarify “results vary”.
- Reviews/testimonials should be real and permissioned.

## Trust & ownership

- Clearly state: client owns ad account + assets; agency gets access via roles.
- Publish privacy basics for lead forms (what you collect, why, retention, how to request deletion).
